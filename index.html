About Us

Wild Circus was founded 1 years ago by Wild Code School funders. After their learning adventure, they wanted to enjoy people using an other way.

See our attractive prices and join to see the best entertainment ever !

The enchanting shows follow each other but are never alike. Only excellence continues. The masters of the place watch over it; they are committed to always rhyming excellence with elegance and flamboyance!
